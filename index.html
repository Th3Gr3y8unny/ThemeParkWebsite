Location

Erupt-O-World is located in the beautiful city of Volcano Vista, just a short drive from downtown. Follow these directions to visit us:

  1. Take Highway 123 to the Volcano Vista exit
  2. Turn right onto Main Street
  3. Turn left onto Erupt-O-World Drive
  4. Follow the signs to the main entrance
