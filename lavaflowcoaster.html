Experience the thrill of the Lava Flow Coaster

About the Lava Flow Coaster

The Lava Flow Coaster is a high-speed roller coaster that takes you through a series of twists and turns as you hurtle through a fiery landscape. With breathtaking views and heart-pumping action, this ride is not to be missed!

Height Requirements

Riders must be at least 48 inches tall to ride the Lava Flow Coaster.

Safety Measures

The Lava Flow Coaster is designed with safety in mind. All riders must wear a seatbelt and follow the ride operator's instructions at all times.

Where to Find the Lava Flow Coaster

The Lava Flow Coaster can be found in the heart of Erupt-O-World, just follow the signs!
